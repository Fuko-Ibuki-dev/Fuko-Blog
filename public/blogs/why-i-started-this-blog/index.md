## Why a blog?

这个 blog 对我来说，更像是一种记录。  
This blog is a way for me to document things.

记录我做过的事情，整理我的思绪，也帮助我把一些模糊的想法慢慢理清。  
To capture what I’ve done, organize my thoughts, and slowly make sense of ideas that are still unclear.

写下来，本身就是一种思考。  
Writing itself is a form of thinking.

## Why the name "Fuko"?

这个名字的来源，其实很简单。  
The origin of this name is actually quite simple.

我深受《Clannad》的影响，它是我最喜欢的动漫/游戏之一。  
I was deeply influenced by *Clannad*, which is one of my favorite anime and games.

所以我选择了其中一个角色的名字——伊吹风子（Ibuki Fuko）。  
So I chose the name of one of its characters — Ibuki Fuko.

她的存在本身就带着一种很特别的感觉。  
There’s something uniquely memorable about her presence.

某种程度上，这个名字也代表了我对这种感觉的共鸣。  
In a way, this name reflects my connection to that feeling.
